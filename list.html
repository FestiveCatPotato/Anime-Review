Psycho Pass

Background Info
Psycho Pass is a Japanese cyberpunk psychological thriller anime produced by Production I.G released in 2012 to critical acclaim in both Japan and the U.S.A. Three seasons and three movies have been released as of writing, with some confusion about whether or not there will be a season 4.
